Welcome to my vlog! My name is Miguel and I'm a climber based in Orange County, CA. I started climbing in 2014 and have been hooked ever since. I boulder, sport climb, and gym climb currently. Hopefully someday I will get into trad and big wall climbing as well. I started this vlog to share the adventures I have gone on and my climbing experiences with anyone who would like to watch and hopefully get some motivation from. Check out the VLOGS link or the GALLERY link above to see the footage captured! Feel free to comment, ask questions, or reach out to me!
