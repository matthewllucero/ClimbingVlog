Welcome to my vlog! My name is Miguel and I'm a climber based in Orange County, CA. I started climbing in 2014 and was hooked ever since. I boulder, sport climb, and gym climb currently and hopefully someday will get into trad and big wall climbing as well. I started this vlog to share the adventures I have gone on and my climbing experiences with anyone who would like to watch and hopefully get some motivation from. Feel free to comment, question, or reach out to me!
